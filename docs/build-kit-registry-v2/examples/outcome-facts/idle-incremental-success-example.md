## Why no recipe change is automatically made

A **single success example** with one user follow-up does **not** meet the evidence threshold for automatic or semi-automatic recipe mutation. Per ADR-0018:

- Outcome facts inform critique; they do not trigger edits.
- Hermes may **propose** changes; it must not apply them.
- **Maintainer review** and normal git merge are required for any YAML change.
- Repeated follow-up patterns or validation/preview failures would be needed before treating this as a recipe gap.

This example stops at “possible future guidance note” — no patch is implied or authorized.
